As of March 18th, 2023, there are 580,466 Homeless people in America according to worldpopulationreview.com. Also as of March 18th, 2023, 336 million people live in America, this means ~0.17% of the population is homeless in America. And with that ~0.17% of the population 35% of them (according to wikipedia). Have no form of shelter. On average homeless people only make $10-$30 daily which is barely enough to live in america. This is also nowhere near enough to afford any form of permanent housing which at its cheapest in america is ~$760 a month in rent.
